Frost Giants are icy, high-level enemies found on Hifrost Peak, where the cold winds and harsh terrain match their frosty demeanor. Known for their strength and resilience, they are a staple challenge for experienced adventurers.

One Frost Giant also appears in the Undercroft Dungeon, dropping the Undercroft Key required to exit the 12th room, along with Giant Bones.

Multiple Frost Giants can be found in Cairn Dungeon, which is accessible by climbing the ladder in the Cairn bank.
