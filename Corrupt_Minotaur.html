Corrupt Minotaurs are powerful and aggressive creatures found deep within the Wastelands. One Corrupt Minotaur also resides in the Undercroft Dungeon, where it drops the Undercroft Key—required to exit the 16th room—as well as Bones. These enemies are known for their brute strength and are not to be taken lightly.
