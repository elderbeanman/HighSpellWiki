Skeletal Mages are undead spellcasters found in the dungeon west of Stone Henge, which requires a pickaxe and level 40 Mining to access. A Wizard's Cauldron is located nearby, hinting at their magical origins. These enemies also appear in the Wastelands, where they continue to cast deadly spells from a distance.

One Skeletal Mage can be found in the Undercroft Dungeon, where it drops the Undercroft Key needed to exit the 5th room, along with Bones. Their magical attacks make them a dangerous foe for unprepared adventurers.
