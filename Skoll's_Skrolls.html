Skoll's Skrolls is located in level 20 of the Wastelands and operated by Skoll. Despite the name, the shop deals exclusively in scrolls, offering a large stock of commonly used Magic scrolls. Due to the remote and dangerous location, it’s primarily visited by prepared players looking to stock up in bulk.

The path to Skoll's Skrolls is guarded by several high-level aggressive NPCs, so it's recommended to bring strong armor and a full inventory of food. Make sure to also carry teleport scrolls in case you need to escape.
