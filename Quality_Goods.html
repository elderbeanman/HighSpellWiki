Quality Goods is located near Banton and operated by the Traveling Merchant. Despite its simple name, the shop offers a wide variety of items ranging from basic combat supplies to powerful higher-end gear.

This shop is notable for carrying rare items like coronium equipment and specialty potions, making it a valuable stop for players looking to gear up or stock uncommon resources.
