General Store
Description We'll pretty much buy anything
Owner Shop Owner

General Stores are found in various locations across the world of HighSpell and all function the same way. These shops primarily offer basic tools and supplies, while also serving as a convenient place to sell off unwanted items.

While General Stores will buy almost anything, players—especially newer ones—should be aware that most items are worth more when traded to other players, or converted to Coins by using Aurum Minor / Majora. In many cases, it's better to hold onto items or sell them at a specialized shop (e.g. helmets → Hank's Helmets) rather than settling for the lower return from a General Store.
