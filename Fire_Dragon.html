Fire Dragons are high-level, powerful enemies located inside the volcano on Dragonsmoke Island. They become accessible after completing the Dragonsmoke Island quest. Players can safely fight them using Range or Magic from behind the rocks scattered throughout the area.

They can also be found in level 20 of the Wastelands, reachable by descending the ladder next to Skoll's Skrolls.

One Fire Dragon is also located in the Undercroft Dungeon, serving as the final challenge in the 22nd room. It drops the Undercroft Key required to exit the dungeon, along with Giant Bones and Fire Dragonleather. Known for their devastating fire attacks and tanky defenses, Fire Dragons are a true endgame test for seasoned adventurers.
