Map

The Rage Obelisk is located in level 5 of the Wastelands, towards mid-western side. It is the sixth obelisk to be used in the Enchanting skill; and will create one or more Rage Scroll for every Oak Scroll the player has in their inventory, granting 11 XP per Oak Scroll converted.

Players with at least level 40 Magic can cast the Rage Obelisk Teleport spell to quickly travel to the obelisk.
