Wizards are powerful magic users located in the Wastelands, with a shortcut leading to them through Hifrost Peak. They cast accurate magic spells that deal significantly more consistent damage than melee attacks, making them a challenging foe for unprepared players.

Pickpocketing[edit]

At level 74 Crime, players can pickpocket Wizards for 200 XP. On failure, the player is stunned for 6 Ticks (3.6s), takes up to 3 damage, and the Wizard exclaims, "What on earth are you doing?"
