Map

The entrance to the Undercroft Dungeon is located at level 10 within the Wastelands. While the dungeon itself is not PvP-enabled, the surrounding entrances are—so caution is still advised when entering or exiting.

High combat stats and solid equipment are recommended, as the dungeon is filled with dangerous enemies. Throughout the dungeon, players can find several lootable chests. However, it's important to note that if you save your Undercroft Key for the fifth and final chest—after beating all 22 rooms in one run—you'll have a chance at the best possible loot. Using your key on an earlier chest will reset your progress.
