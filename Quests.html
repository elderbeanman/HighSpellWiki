Quests

From HighSpell Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Quests are one-time tasks that reward players with various benefits upon completion. They often come with requirements, such as specific skill levels or the ability to survive challenging combat encounters. While quests are usually completed for their valuable rewards, they also offer a fun and engaging break from the usual gameplay grind.

As of now, there are only four quests available in HighSpell.

List of Quests[edit]

  • Dragonsmoke Island
  • Leprechaun Flute
  • The Lost Trumpet
  • Sato's Trials
